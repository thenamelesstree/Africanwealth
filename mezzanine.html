MEZZANINE BetaChain

The MEZZANINE betaChain is the first child chain of Africanwealth and is used for the management of accounts and other platform-wide applications, systems and tools. It is also an open chain, i.e. it does not require any special permissions to transact on.

The betaChain carries some of the distributed applications built into Africanwealth that do not necessarily need regulatory control or compliance. Also, distributed applications found on the MEZZANINE betaChain may also be found on other permissioned chains but under certain conditions and regulations depending on applications and administration.

As the main betaChain, AfricanWealth is thus the socio-economic hub of the Africanwealth Blockchain.

MEZZANINE Transaction Fees

All fees for transactions executed on the MEZZANINE chain are paid in the chain's own transaction fees deducted from the sender account's transaction fee balance. These fees are essential for users to access and make use of the chain's distributed applications other transactional services.
